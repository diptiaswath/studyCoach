Plot interpretationVisual context still hurts verdict (−4.4pp at 32B, −10.5pp at 8B)Trend reading remains challenging; model gets distracted by visual patterns

Omission errors at 32BVisual context hurts verdict by −26.7pp (worse than 8B's −6.7pp)Larger model overweights visual information for completeness judgments
Why text-only "wins" verdict (but for the wrong reasons): In C1, the model evaluates using pre-trained knowledge and internal consistency — plausibility checking, not verification against the figure. It can't confirm "BERT achieves 84.6%" — it just judges if the claim sounds reasonable. Multimodal classifies worse but explains better because it actually uses the figure. This is why scaling matters: we want the model to verify claims against the actual figure, not guess based on training knowledge.
