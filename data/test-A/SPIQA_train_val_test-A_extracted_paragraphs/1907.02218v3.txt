We presented composable sampling sketches for weighted sampling of unaggregated data tailored to a concave sublinear function of the frequencies of keys. We experimentally demonstrated the simplicity and efficacy of our design: Our sketch size is nearly optimal in that it is not much larger than the final sample size, and the estimate quality is close to that provided by a weighted sample computed directly over the aggregated data.
