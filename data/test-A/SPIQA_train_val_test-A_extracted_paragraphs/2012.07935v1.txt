We study a basic algorithmic meta-question: given nрқ‘ӣn independent random variables, select kрқ‘ҳk of them, with the objective of maximizing the expected largest value, and/or the expected second highest value.We are motivated by the following applications:Search engineGiven a search query, the search engine has to return kрқ‘ҳk results of nрқ‘ӣn candidates. In their seminal WAND paper, Broder et. al. In this work, we extend the seminal ideas of [KR18] in multiple directions.
