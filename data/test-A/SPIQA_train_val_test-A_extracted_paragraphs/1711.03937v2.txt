In this paper, we propose variance reduced stochastic compositional proximal gradient method (VRSC-PG) for composition problem with nonsmooth regularization penalty. Finally, we apply our method to two different applications, portfolio management and reinforcement learning. Experimental results show that our method always has the best performance in different cases and verify the conclusions of theoretical analysis.
