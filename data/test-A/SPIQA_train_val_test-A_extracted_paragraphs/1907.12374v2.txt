To investigate further we compare MMD and GAN in distribution matching via a synthetic experiment. We show that both approaches perform well for low dimensional Dirichlet distribution yet MMD performs much better than GAN in higher dimensional setting.Our setup is as follows. 100,000100000100,000 input vectors are drawn from a 2D spherical Gaussian distribution.The encoder network consists of two hidden layers with 2 neurons in each layer and a 2D output layer with softmax. The goal is to train the encoder network so that the output appears to come from a 2D Dirichlet prior distribution of parameter 0.1.
