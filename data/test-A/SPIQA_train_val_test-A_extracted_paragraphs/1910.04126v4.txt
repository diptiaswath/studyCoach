The above results exhibit a sharp divide between fast and coarse algorithms to slow and accurate ones.In practical nearest neighbor search system, both types of algorithms are often combined sequentially as aВ pipeline (e.g., [SZ03, JDS08, JDS10]).First, a fast and coarse method is applied to all points, pruning most of them; then a slower and more accurate method is applied to the surviving points, pruning them further; and so on, until finally exact computation is performed on a small number of surviving points.In particular, [KSKW15] employ such a pipeline for the Word Mover Distance, which combines Mean, R-WMD, and exact W1subscriptрқ‘Ҡ1W_{1} computation.
