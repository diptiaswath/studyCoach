The problem of optimizing a black-box function fūĚĎďf with as few evaluations of fūĚĎďf as possible arises in many scientific and industrial fields such as computer experiments (Joneset¬†al., 1998; Richet et¬†al., 2013) or automatic selection of hyperparameters in machine learning (Bergstra et¬†al., 2011). First, even if the results of Section¬†2 could be easily extended to pseudo-metric spaces as in Munos (2014) and related works, our other results are finite-dimensional and exploit the normed space structure.
