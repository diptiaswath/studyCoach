Dimensionality reduction using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) is widely usedfor all types of data analysis, classification and clustering. In recent years,numerous subspace clustering methods have been proposed, to address the shortcomingsof traditional PCA methods. The work on Robust PCA by Candès et al. [6]is one of the most influential papers on the subject, which sparked a large researchinterest from various fields including computer vision. Applications include, but are notlimited to,rigid and non-rigid structure-from-motion [4, 1],photometric stereo [2] and optical flow [12].
