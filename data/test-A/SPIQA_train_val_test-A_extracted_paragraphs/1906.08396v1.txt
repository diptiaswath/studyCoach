Recovering a structured signal from a set of linear observations appears in many applications in areas ranging from finance to biology, and from imaging to signal processing. More formally, the goal is to recover an unknown vector 𝐱0∈ℝnsubscript𝐱0superscriptℝ𝑛\mathbf{x}_{0}\in\mathbb{R}^{n}, from observations of the form yi=𝐚i𝖳​𝐱0subscript𝑦𝑖superscriptsubscript𝐚𝑖𝖳subscript𝐱0y_{i}=\mathbf{a}_{i}^{\mathsf{T}}\mathbf{x}_{0}, for i=1,…,m𝑖1…𝑚i=1,\dots,m.
