The problem of distribution-free inference has been studied extensively in the context of predictive inference, where the goalis to provide a confidence band for the response value Yn+1subscript𝑌𝑛1Y_{n+1} given a new feature vector Xn+1subscript𝑋𝑛1X_{n+1}.
