We have presented a generalized PCA that focuses on maximizing the sum of an arbitrary convex function of principal components. We have presented a simple gradient ascent algorithm for the non-kernel version of the problem, and devised a simple recurrent neural network to solve the general case. We have observed that our generalized PCA outperforms the existing Lpsuperscript𝐿𝑝L^{p}-norm based PCA methods in several scenarios.
