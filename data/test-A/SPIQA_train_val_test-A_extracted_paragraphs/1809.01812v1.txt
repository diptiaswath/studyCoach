In this paper we have analyzed binary and ranking variants of NCE for estimation of conditional models p​(y|x;θ)𝑝conditional𝑦𝑥𝜃p(y|x;\theta). The ranking-based variant is consistent for a broader class of models than the binary-based algorithm.
